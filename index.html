My name is Valeria Madrid-Marquez, I am twenty years old, I attend the University of California, Riverside. Currently I am majoring in Sociology in hopes of getting into the Social Worker field. A social worker has a very important job. Social workers help children and families in need of food, homes, and health care. They help children have a brighter future. There are many different types of social work. There are mental health, child and family, elementary, and high school social workers. There are advantages to being a social worker. One example is social workers get to save children. They take the child or children out of an abusive home, weather that is mental abuse, or physical abuse. Social workers also find ways to help the family if they have an illness and can not take care of the child or children, or have a problem with drugs or alcohol and are unfit to care for the child or children.It has always been a dream to be able to help families and most importantly children when in need of assistance. In order to fully reach my goal to become a social worker, I am currently a student intern at San Bernardino High School preparing high school students for college readiness. Besides assisting them through their college readiness, I help them with any other courses they need assistance with such as math, science, english, or any art course. Being a student intern has helped me give myself an insight of some things that my career will focus. In order to become a successful social worker I must have good communication with other people. When people start recognizing that you have great communication rather than just giving them single word answers they have more trust in you. Which is one of the major things I need to focus on when I am dealing with real world situations. It has never been an easy job to be a social worker as for some cases can get very sensitive and emotional, which is why I have to be strong and assist these families or children from any toxicity. Being a social worker is not easy, it is filled with plenty of obstacles for others and especially myself as I will be going through any hard journey they are in need of assistance, but it is a job I am willing to take and assist those in need.
